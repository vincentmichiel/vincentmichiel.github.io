BIOGRAPHY

The Origin

Born and raised in the Netherlands, Owen Bryce is an English-speaking singer-songwriter and producer crafting dreamy folk indie pop infused with freedom, nostalgia, and optimism. His music feels like a gentle breeze-comforting and hopeful, yet unafraid to embrace melancholy.

With subtle, reflective lyrics and warm melodies, he invites listeners to pause, embrace life, and accept that it's okay to feel lost or sad sometimes. In the end, everything will be alright. Currently studying at the Rock Academy, Owen continues to refine his sound and grow as an artist.

He draws inspiration from the stories people share with him and his own experiences, weaving them into his music in a unique and heartfelt way. Through authentic storytelling, he touches his listeners and takes them on a dreamy journey.
